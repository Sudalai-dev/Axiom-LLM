## Executive Summary

Axiom (AI-Driven Inference IoT Orchestration Model) is a proprietary
enterprise AI Engineering Platform designed to function as the cognitive
operating system for AIoT ecosystems.

It implements an 8-layer Cognitive Intelligence Engine:

1.  Perception
2.  Capture
3.  Normalization
4.  Enrichment
5.  Inference & Synthesis
6.  Reasoning
7.  Prescription
8.  Experience
